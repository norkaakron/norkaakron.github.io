Bio

Norka was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada where she lived for eight years before moving to the Bay area.
She has studied and worked with a wide range of digital media platforms and programs.
Norka works primarily in digital media art but also likes to go back to traditional drawing and animation.

Her most recent exhibition was named “I & I,” in May 2019.
Featuring image transfer pieces mounted onto frosted plastic, suspended by frosted plastic rods.
She also took part in the Cadre club at SJSU, this is a club dedicated to all aspects of digital media art.
In the club Norka was an active part of the VR team.
Her duties included: 3d modeling, image editing, and texture editing, as well as assisting fellow club members, and table sitting for our booth at Maker Faire.

Norka received an AA in Film/TV:Animation Emphasis from De Anza College
and graduated San Jose State University May 2019: Bachelor's of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art.
She is a dean's scholar with a gpa of 3.734.
